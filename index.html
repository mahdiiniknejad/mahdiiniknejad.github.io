Research focus

  • Credit rationing & financing constraints: why viable SMEs get rejected, and how frictions vary across firms and conditions.
  • Trade finance innovation: reducing operational risk and rejections through better verification and settlement.
  • P2P / marketplace lending: alternative credit channels, risk modelling, and information asymmetry.
  • Blockchain & tokenisation: real-world assets (RWA), stablecoins, and practical use-cases in finance.

Teaching

  • Teaching Assistant — Financial Data Science (Trading & Risk Management)
  • Topics delivered: blockchain foundations, tokenisation, DeFi risks, and applied fintech cases.

Projects in progress

  • SME credit frictions map: identifying and ranking the binding constraints (information, collateral, risk environment).
  • Trade document verification: AI-based checks + workflow design to reduce fraud and approval delays.
  • Settlement/collateral mechanisms: how faster settlement or tokenised collateral can change pricing and approval.
  • P2P lending research notes: platform design challenges, screening, default risk, and borrower outcomes.
